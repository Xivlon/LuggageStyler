Who Are We?

We're Movers of a Different Kind

Luggsters, Inc. was created from the roots of a family-owned business to meet a growing demand in the travel industry: fast, dependable, and secure luggage transport. Whether it's tourists needing a place to store their bags after hotel check-out or cruise passengers eager to explore without being weighed down by their suitcases — Luggsters delivers the freedom to travel hands-free.

Our mission is to create a trusted driver network that provides peace of mind for travelers—and opportunity for you.

What is a Luggster?

A Luggster is more than just a driver. You're a luggage ambassador—a trusted solution when travelers ask, "What do I do with my bags all day?" You provide peace of mind, freedom, and convenience, becoming a dependable part of someone's journey. Together, Luggsters are changing the game—setting a new standard for customer service and delivering the kind of experience travelers will never forget.
